About US

Köhler & Wernoth LED lighting projects provide comprehensive LED lighting indoors and outdoors. We also offer the associated planning services, determine the LED funding options and take care of the complete processing of the LED lighting projects, including the coordination of the specialist companies.

Köhler & Wernoth LED light projects were founded by Frank Wernoth and Gerrit Köhler. They are the bright minds behind the company and have decades of experience in lighting technology, financial planning, sales and marketing.
